know About FAI Media

We are Riyad, Khairul, and Khaled — three friends who started this channel on October 8, 2023 with a shared passion for meaningful content.

What began as a space for Islamic knowledge and reflections has recently grown into a platform focused on science and educational videos. Our goal is to make complex ideas clear, relevant, and enjoyable for everyone.

We Produce well-researched, easy-to-follow videos that blend curiosity with clarity.
We Create content that moves between faith-based topics and modern scientific subjects — always with respect, accuracy, and care. Aim to inspire viewers to learn, think, and explore.

We have 55+ videos. 170+ subscribers. 10,000+ total views. These milestones are small but meaningful steps — and your feedback keeps us improving. ❤️

Why We Make Videos

We believe knowledge is empowering. Whether it’s a lesson from tradition or a discovery from science, we want to present it in a way that's useful, honest, and engaging. Teamwork, curiosity, and responsibility guide everything we publish.

What to Expect Next

More science-focused content: clear explanations, practical examples, and approachable deep-dives. We’ll keep the respectful tone that defined our earlier Islamic content while building a bridge to modern topics.

About Our Website

FAI Media is continuously evolving. This website will be regularly updated with new features, tools, and content to better serve our growing community.
In the near future, we plan to introduce FAI Studio a dedicated space for creative production, research-driven media, and original storytelling. Alongside this, a Resources section will be added, offering educational materials, references, and practical learning tools.
We are also working on launching an Official FAI Shop, where users will be able to access curated Digital products and Merchandise connected to our content and vision.
Additional features, improvements, and interactive sections will be rolled out over time. Our goal is to build a platform that goes beyond publishing—creating a complete ecosystem for learning, creativity, and meaningful engagement.

Upcoming Content

FAI Media began its journey with Islamic-focused content, aiming to present meaningful ideas with respect, clarity, and responsibility. After this initial phase, we took a long-term creative break to reflect, research, and redefine our direction.
Around Mid-2025, we returned with a renewed vision—shifting our focus toward science-based content and trending real-world topics. This transition allowed us to explore modern issues while maintaining the thoughtful tone that defined our early work.
Looking ahead to 2026, we are planning to produce several Documentary-style projects. These will cover both local (domestic) and international subjects, with an emphasis on in-depth research, factual accuracy, and impactful storytelling.
Our upcoming content will continue to evolve, bridging knowledge, context, and relevance for a broader audience.
